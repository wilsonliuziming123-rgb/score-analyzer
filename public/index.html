Our mission

Data should widen care, not narrow it.

Every score belongs to a person: someone with a story, pressure, confidence, fatigue, momentum, and a future that cannot be reduced to a number. Score Analyzer exists to make performance data more humane, so classrooms can notice patterns without losing sight of people.

We believe analysis should help communities respond with fairness: more support where students are unseen, more challenge where students are ready, and more context when a single cutoff fails to describe real learning.

See the whole room

Statistics should reveal who is thriving, who is drifting, and where the group needs care before small gaps become social gaps.

Respect different systems

Standard scores, curves, and AP bands carry different meanings. A humane tool should adapt instead of forcing one definition of success.

Turn insight into responsibility

The point is not to rank students faster. The point is to help teachers, tutors, and learners decide what support should happen next.
